How to play

  1. Each game is 6 rounds — one per hurricane-season month, June through November (each from a random year). Watch how the shear relaxes and the storms strengthen toward the Aug–Oct peak, then fade by November. The map shows the real ERA5 environment for that month.
  2. Four candidate TC seeds drop onto the map (the colored pins).
  3. Pick the seed you think will spin up into the storm with the most Accumulated Cyclone Energy (ACE) — a measure of a storm's total power over its whole life, so a long-lived hurricane scores more than a brief intense one.
  4. We integrate every seed forward through the real winds, shear, and ocean until it dissipates — then reveal who was right and score you against the best possible pick.

Warm water + low shear + a long track over open ocean = big ACE. Land and shear are storm-killers.
